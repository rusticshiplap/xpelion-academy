Buying Guide

What Every K-12 Student Needs

🖩 Calculators

Grades 6–8: Scientific calculator (Casio fx-991EX) — handles algebra, stats, and basic trig.

Grades 9–12: TI-84 Plus CE — required for SAT, ACT, AP exams, and most state tests.

College: TI-Nspire CX II or Desmos (free, browser-based).

📚 Textbooks

For AP courses, use the official College Board approved textbook for your subject. For extra practice, Art of Problem Solving (AoPS) books are unmatched for building deep math intuition.

For college, check your school's library — many textbooks are freely available digitally through your institution.

💻 Technology

iPad + Apple Pencil: Best combo for annotating PDFs and digital note-taking. Pair with Notability or GoodNotes.

Noise-canceling headphones are one of the highest-ROI study investments — reducing distraction measurably improves focus and retention.
